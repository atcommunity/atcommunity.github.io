Ahmedabad Tinkering Community

Innovation & Sustainability

The Ahmedabad Tinkering Community (ATC) is a platform dedicated to fostering education, creativity, and sustainability through hands-on innovation. Our goal is to create micro tinkering ecosystems across the city, enabling communities to collaborate, learn, and build together.

We organize STEM learning programs, innovation challenges, and hands-on workshops to promote curiosity-driven learning. Our members participate in various activities such as robotics, electronics, coding, and sustainability projects.

If you're passionate about technology, sustainability, or education, join us today to collaborate with like-minded innovators!
